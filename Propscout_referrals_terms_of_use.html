PROPSCOUT LIMITED REFERRALS TERMS OF AGREEMENT


INTRODUCTION

This agreement establishes a referral arrangement between Propscout Ltd. (“Propscout”) and Real estate professionals, eg property managers and real estate agents and brokers (“Broker”), including any agents operating under the Broker.

Propscout Limited has created a system to generate and deliver quality referrals to real estate professionals . A ‘referral’, on for sale properties, is defined as any client or co-client that is delivered to the brokerage partner and has indicated the need and desire for a specific real estate transaction within the subsequent 24 months.

During this 24 month period, a referral fee is owed to Propscout Limited for all real estate transactions facilitated for this client, regardless of whether the client was withdrawn from the agent or agency's pipeline. The referral is property of Propscout Limited and we reserve the right to rescind the referral at any time.

For for-rent properties, Propscout Limited sends information on renters who have expressed interest on the rental property advertised, to property managers or landlords . When the prospective renter elects to stay in the rental property, Propscout Limited will be owed a commission from the resultant transaction by the landlord or property manager.

PROPSCOUT LIMITED Referral Policies

Competing Referral Programs

If a brokerage receives the same client from multiple referral sources, PROPSCOUT LTD practices a "first in" policy. If the brokerage has received a referral for the same client from another lead source, then Propscout Limited will request verification that the referral was received first from another source. Otherwise, Propscout Limited reserves the right to request payment of the referral fee upon a successful closing with the client.

Voluntary Participation

Broker’s participation in Propscout service is voluntary and can be terminated by either party at any time with written notice. However, any Referrals made prior to such termination are still bound by this agreement, and referral fees (calculated per the above) will be due upon close of any transactions resulting from such Referrals.
